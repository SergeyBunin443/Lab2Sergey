How to Make Grilled Cheese

Ingredients you'll need:

  • 2 pieces of bread
  • 2 pats of butter
  • 1 slice of monterey jack cheese
  • 1 slice of aged cheddar cheese

Process:

  • Fetch yourself a fat-bottom skillet
  • Heat the skillet over medium heat
  • Butter one side of each slice of bread
  • Place one slice of the buttered bread, buttered side down, into the skillet
  • Quickly place each slice of cheese on the bread, being sure to cover the entire surface area
  • Place the other slice of bread, butter side up, on top of the cheese
  • Wait approximately 1 to 2 minutes (or till the bread touching the skillet is a golden brown)
  • Flip the sandwich over with some awesome pizazz and cook the other side till golden brown (approximately 1 to 2 minutes)
  • . Eat with ketchup, or just enjoy the plain crispy goodness of your grilled cheese

    Note:

    Grilled cheese is great on a grill, but for a quicker method, try using a clamshell panini press. The result will be much quicker, allowing you to make many more for you and your friends.

    How to Make French Fries

    Crispy and delicious!

    Ingredients:

    • 4 large potatoes
    • a pot of boiling water
    • a pot of oil heated to 350 degrees fahrenheit

    Process:

    1. Cut the potatoes into strips, roughly a 1/4" by 1/4"
    2. Place the potato strips into the boiling water for approximately 2 minutes
    3. Remove them and let them dry, or pat them dry
    4. Once dry, place the strips into the hot oil
    5. Cook for approximately 8 minutes or until golden brown and crispy
    6. Serve on a plate with ketchup, vinegar, or if you're a complete weirdo, mayonnaise
